Guessing Game

Our computer has choosen a number from 1 to 100. You have 10 guesses to pick the number. Enter your guess in the box below and click the Make Guess button. The computer will tell you whether your guess is high, low, or right! Keeping guessing until you run out of guesses or hit the correct number.
